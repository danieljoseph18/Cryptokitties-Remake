Dan's Kitty Dapp

Welcome to Dan's Kitty Dapp!


About

This Dapp provides a fun, interactive experience where users can customize and purchase their own original Kitty NFT's.

Kitties will be appended to the Ethereum Blockchain, so ownership will be confirmed, as well as all individual information about the Kitty being open-source and visible.

Kitties can be bred with one another to create new unique variations, which can accrue in value overtime depending on style and rarity.

Gen 0 cats are regarded as the rarest, with rarity decreasing as generation increases.

You can create custom Kitties in the Kitty Creator. You are able to breed your owned Kitties in the Kitty Breeder. You can also view your collection of Kitties in the Kittylogue.
